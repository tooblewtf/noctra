Noctra v1.0.0 – Horror-Themed Minecraft Shader

Welcome to the shadows…

Noctra v1.0.0 plunges your Minecraft world into a chilling, horror-inspired atmosphere. Perfect for haunted mansions, dark forests, and eerie night adventures, this shader transforms light and shadow into your new nightmare.

Features:

Creepy Lighting & Shadows – Deep, oppressive shadows that hide secrets in every corner.

Blood-Red Sunsets & Moonlight – Atmospheric skies that set the tone for suspense.

Fog of Dread – Thick, unsettling fog that creeps along the landscape.

Enhanced Horror Ambience – Subtle visual effects to make every step feel tense.

Installation:

Download the shader pack.

Place it in your shaderpacks folder.

Select it in the Minecraft shader options.

Brace yourself…

Step into the darkness. Explore the unknown. Survive the night.
